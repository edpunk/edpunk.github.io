Collectively, Personal Information and Non-Personal Information shall be referred to as "Information" in this policy.

The primary purpose of collecting Information is to provide our Services, to communicate with you, or to make our Services better. By providing Information of others to the Company, you represent that you have authority to do so. We disclaim responsibility for the information of others that you provide to us in the course of your use of the Services.

Let's go through the information that we collect.

2.1 INFORMATION COLLECTED FROM YOU DIRECTLY

We receive Personal Information directly from you when you provide us with such Personal Information, including without limitation the following:

  • Account Information, such as name, email address, username, or display picture;
  • Information to enable us to check and verify your identity, e.g. your date of birth, mark-sheets, school ID card, Passport/Aadhar/Voter ID details;

  • Your feedback, preferences and settings like reviews or ratings, syllabus and course preferences, account settings, app settings; and
  • Information provided to us through our service, interaction with our customer service, participation in surveys or marketing promotions.
2.2 INFORMATION COLLECTED AUTOMATICALLY

We also collect some information automatically through our Services or using third party service providers. This information includes:

  • Log information: Like most online service providers, we collect information that web browsers, mobile devices, and servers typically make available, including the browser type, IP address, unique device identifiers, language preference, referring site, the date and time of access, device name, device size, other device information, operating system, mobile network information, crash dump, and other statistics.
  • Usage information: We collect information (directly and using third party service provides) about your usage of our Services which includes both App and Website. We use this information to, for example, provide our Services to you, get insights on how people use our Services so that we can make our Services better, and understand and make predictions about user retention.
  • Location information: We collect location information of our users, which is not personally identifiable, using third party service providers. We collect and use this information to, for example, calculate how many people visit our Services from certain geographic regions.
  • Stored information: We may access information stored on your mobile device via our App. We access this stored information through your device operating system’s permissions. No stored information is accessed unless the user choses to upload it via the App.
  • Information from cookies & other technologies: Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are commonly used as anonymous unique identifiers. These are sent to your browser from the websites that you visit and are stored on your device's internal memory. This Service does not use these "cookies" explicitly. However, the third party service providers may use "cookies" to collect information.
